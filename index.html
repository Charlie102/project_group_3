COMMON NAIL INDUSTRY TERMS

Gradient

This is a word used quite commonly in fine arts, by definition, it is the transformation of one color into another.

In the nail industry, this color transition is done vertically downward along the length of the nail.

Gradient nail art is very unique, and beautiful, just by creating color changes on the nails, no need to attach stones, charms or fancy nail art, you can still own a beautiful, outstanding nail set. It's also very unique and out of the ordinary.

Shadow

If we have ombre hair dye, we also have ombre nail art.

This is the term in the nail industry for a pretty and unique nail art based on color variations. Ombre is a combination of different shades of the same color, usually going from light to dark.

This type of paint is also done similar to the Gradient paint (the color changes according to the length of the nail), but slightly different from the Gradient that is Ombre is usually the color transition from one finger to another.

Remember to keep in mind the characteristics of these two nail polish styles so as not to be confused.

Color blocking

Color blocking is a nail industry term for nail art that creates a solid block of color. This style is created by dividing the nail into several separate sections.

Depending on the customer's preferences that shape, it can be squares, rectangles, diagonal stripes, triangles... Then you mix the paint colors for each part. It is possible to use many different colors to create uniqueness, all based on the creativity of each person will create different nail styles.

Water marbling

Water marbling is the term for the method by which you will drip 2 (or more) paint colors into the water to create a design with an extremely unique paint color blend.

Then, you dip your nails directly into the water so that the paint rotates into the nail, creating a beautiful and wonderful nail set with a wave-like effect.

Tape Manicure

In nail industry terms, Tape manicure is a slang term and refers to a nail polish technique in which Scotch tape is applied to a part of the nail that you do not intend to paint on. This technique should be used for color blocking or creating a lightning bolt design.

You can also use separate nail art striping tape, which is very narrow, to create stripes and slimmer designs.

Stamping /Konad

Regarding stamping, this is a term in the nail industry, also referring to a nail polish technique that requires the use of stamping pads and nail polish, allowing you to stamp images before designing. onto the nail.

Konad is a term named after a famous author/distributor of nail stamping tools, supplying large quantities of nail polish and stamping dies to the world nail industry.

Saran Wrap/ Cling Wrap Manicure

Saran wrap is a slang term for a nail polish technique that requires 2 (or more) bottles of nail polish and some Saran resin or adhesive film and can be used in the process of reducing or adding polish.

Here, we will show you this nail polish technique.

For a reduced nail look, you apply base coat and wait for it to dry, then apply a second coat. Before the second coat dries, roll a bit of saran into a ball and stick it on. on wet paint, then pull it away. It will pull away a bit of the top coat, creating a textured effect.

Ruffian

In this nail industry terminology, ruffian is a slang term for nail polish that combines 2 (or more) colors. The first shade is coated in the usual way. The second tint is slightly indented, starting a little further from the base layer, so the first color is still visible.

Cat eye paint

In addition to the traditional nail polish styles, in recent times, a new term in the nail industry has been popularized for a rather special type of paint - cat eye paint.

Cat's eye gel polish is a line of professional salon products made in gel technique.

The product is the perfect combination of gel polish and creative emulsion, creating a cat's eye effect with soft, feminine colors that are easy to mix and match.

With this paint style, we will combine cat eye gel polish with matte polish or top gloss to create a fancy effect. And when using cat eye paint, we need an additional tool that is a magnet to create a cat's eye effect.

Nail trends that "cause fever" around the world

Asymmetrical nail trends-Tokyo, Japan

Asymmetrical nail design is one of the very popular trends in Japan. Japanese women usually love to draw different textures and colors on each hand or even each nail.

Nude nail trends-Melbourne, Australia

Not as colorful as Japanese asymmetrical nails and not as prominent as nail foils in LA, girls from Melbourne often choose a simple nude style, gentle but no less luxurious. When choosing nude nail polish, nail technicians often choose a color that is lighter or darker than the skin color to create a contrast that makes the nails stand out and delicate.

Metallic nail trends-London, UK

London-based nail artist-Stephanie Staunton reveals that metallics are on the rise wind" in her city today. There are many ways to create this stylish nail, which can be used Use metallic paints, glitters on nude or even neon colors to create a light effect sparkling light. In addition, with these "items", you can also unleash your creativity from French island nails back to the very eye-catching geometric nail.

Nail Trends "Typography"-Amsterdam, Netherlands

With a unique and different style, "typographic" is the hottest nail trend in the city. Amsterdam now. Decorating nails with classic letters is a very bold thing, personality and completely new. Arranging them vertically, diagonally or even cluttered is what the Nail artists often choose when designing this nail style. However, before trying them out, make sure Make sure you have a steady hand and maximum attention to detail.

Matte nail trends-Paris, France

In Paris, bright matte nails create a "craze" in the nail industry today. This trend very cool, more suitable for summer than regular polishes. Most of the beauty believers here usually choose any favorite shade, then add a few accessories, simple textures or even Simple nail design but enough to create confidence and youthfulness throughout the week.

Trend "accent nails"-New York, USA

Creating a few highlights on the monochrome nails is the style most beauty followers in New York are pursue in the summer. This is a great choice when you don't want to stand out too much with foil nails or too boring by monochrome nails. Just paint a base color on all nails, then add some additional colors or accessories in some nails and you have perfect "accent nails" hands.
